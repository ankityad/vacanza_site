RULES

ROUND 1

Photgraphy/Doodling

1.Participants have to choose one or both domains between doodling and photography.
2. For photography, you can choose a maximum of 3 domains from the following domains -
• Wildlife
• Architecture
• Travel
• Food
• Culture
And can upload 1 photograph against each of the three chosen domains and tag Computer society of India i.e. a total of maximum 3 photographs can be uploaded.
2.Doodling / Photographs are supposed to be uploaded on Facebook with #csi #vacanza17.
3.Only one member of a team have to upload these photographs on facebook.
4.Team will be judged on the basis of number of likes and shares on their Doodles / Photographs and the points obtained in the quiz.
5.Teams/Participants will be judged on the basis of number of likes and shares on their Doddles / Photographs and the points obtained in the quiz.


Quiz round

1.Participants can give quizzes of both the domains i.e.Tv series and movie quiz by their choice.
2.Participants have to choose one of the given TV series for the quiz -
• Games of Thrones
• Friends
• Flash
AND/OR
Choose one of the given movies for the quiz -
• Star wars
• Titanic
• Marvel cinematic universe
3. Both Quizzes will carry 10 questions each of which is compulsory.
4.Each quiz will be of 2 minutes duration time failure of which may lead to non-consideration of their response.
5.Each participant is allowed to take the quiz only once.
6.Only one member of a team can give the quiz,if both  members participated in quiz then they will be disqualified.

ROUND 2

1. List of 10 household items will be given to each participant.
2. Participants are supposed to collect the maximum possible items and send their clicked photographs to us (on fb) as directed.
3. Each item collected will be equal to 1 point.
4. Also, There will be one bonus question which will Carry 5 points.
5. Bonus question will be optional.
6. Participants have to send the pictures to Computer Society of India Facebook page in the given duration of time only.
7. Photoshoped or Googled photos will not be considered and may lead the participant to disqualification.

ROUND 3

1. Each participant have to upload a dubsmash video enacting a TV advertisement.
2. Participants will be judged on the basis of number of likes and comments on their video.
3. Participants have to send the video to Computer Society of India Facebook page in the given duration of time only.

Key Points

1. Don't forget to tag and or mention Computer Society of India AKGEC's fb page in the photographs and Doodles
2. All the photographs have to be uploaded in a single album.
3. Those participants who haven't liked the page won't be eligible for any award.
